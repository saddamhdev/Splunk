**Splunk** is a **data analytics platform** that collects, indexes, and analyzes **machine-generated data**‚Äîthings like logs, metrics, events, and traces‚Äîfrom servers, apps, networks, and security tools.

### In simple terms

Splunk helps you **answer questions from your data** in real time:

* *Why did my app crash?*
* *Is someone attacking my system?*
* *Why is the server slow right now?*

## How Splunk works (high level)

1. **Forwarders** ‚Üí send logs/events
2. **Indexer** ‚Üí stores & indexes data
3. **Search Head** ‚Üí you search + build dashboards

Think of it like **Google Search for system data**.
